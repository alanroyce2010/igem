Project Overview

This molecule represents \[\ce{C6H6} (benzene)\].

This project aims to engineer the diatom Thalassiosira pseudonana to produce the valuable industrial chemical **acetophenone** in a sustainable and environmentally friendly manner.

Diatoms are single-celled photosynthetic **microalgae** abundant in the oceans and known for their efficient CO2 fixation capabilities. By introducing specific genes into their genome, we can modify their natural metabolic pathways to produce desired compounds like acetophenone.

Why Acetophenone?

Acetophenone is a versatile aromatic ketone with various applications, including:

  • and flavoring agent: Used in perfumes, cosmetics, and food additives.
  • Pharmaceutical precursor: Serves as a starting material for the synthesis of various drugs and medications.
  • Industrial solvent: Employed in various industrial processes due to its solvency properties.

Currently, acetophenone is primarily produced from fossil fuels, raising concerns about sustainability and environmental impact. This project offers a promising alternative for eco-friendly production.

Our Approach

We are following a multi-step approach to achieve our goal:

  1. Gene selection and design: Identify and design the necessary genes for the acetophenone biosynthesis pathway.
  2. Transformation: Introduce the designed genes into the diatom **T. pseudonana** using genetic engineering techniques.
  3. Characterization: Evaluate the transformed diatoms for acetophenone production efficiency and optimize growth conditions.
  4. Downstream processing: Develop methods to extract and purify the produced acetophenone.

By successfully completing these steps, we hope to establish a viable biological system for sustainable acetophenone production using engineered diatoms.

Project Impact

This project has the potential to bring about significant positive impacts:

  • Promote sustainable production: Offers an eco-friendly alternative to traditional fossil fuel-based acetophenone production.
  • Reduce environmental impact: Contributes to mitigating climate change by utilizing renewable resources and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.
  • Advance synthetic biology: Provides valuable insights into metabolic engineering of diatoms for the production of valuable compounds.
  • Inspire future generations: Serves as an inspiring example of how synthetic biology can be applied to address global challenges.
